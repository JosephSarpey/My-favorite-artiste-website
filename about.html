ABOUT

Sarkodie, born Michael Owusu Addo, is a trailblazing Ghanaian rapper, songwriter, and entrepreneur, celebrated as one of Africa's most influential music icons. Known for his lightning-fast rap delivery, captivating storytelling, and innovative fusion of African rhythms with modern beats, Sarkodie has set a gold standard in the music industry. From humble beginnings in Tema, Ghana, to gracing international stages, Sarkodie's journey is a testament to passion, talent, and perseverance. His works resonate with diverse audiences, blending profound messages, cultural pride, and infectious energy. As the first Ghanaian to win the BET Best International Flow award, Sarkodie has not only uplifted African hip-hop but also inspired countless artists globally. Beyond music, he is a philanthropist, a family man, and a visionary committed to empowering the youth and advancing African creativity on the world stage. Welcome to the world of Sarkodie—where music meets legacy. With an impressive catalog of award-winning albums and chart-topping hits, Sarkodie continues to redefine the boundaries of African music. His projects, such as Sarkology, Highest, and No Pressure, showcase his versatility—seamlessly blending Afrobeats, hip-hop, and highlife to craft sounds that transcend borders. Sarkodie’s collaborations with international artists like Ace Hood, Wale, and Burna Boy further affirm his role as a bridge between Ghanaian culture and the global music scene. Beyond the mic, Sarkodie is a cultural ambassador, using his platform to highlight issues affecting society and to champion African excellence. Whether through his Sarkcess Music label, initiatives like the Sarkodie Foundation, or his groundbreaking performances, Sarkodie embodies a legacy of artistry, resilience, and cultural pride.

Sarkodie’s influence extends far beyond music, making him a symbol of inspiration for the next generation. As a passionate advocate for education, entrepreneurship, and community development, he consistently uses his voice to drive meaningful change. From empowering young talents through mentorship to spearheading charitable projects that uplift underprivileged communities, Sarkodie’s impact resonates deeply across Africa and beyond. With every beat, lyric, and initiative, he reaffirms his commitment to leaving an indelible mark on the world.
